About Us

Sadhvika Jewellers, founded by Manchiryala Ramesh and Manchiryala Sricharan in 1997, blends heritage with elegance. With decades of expertise, we craft timeless designs inspired by India’s rich goldsmith legacy. Since 1997, Sadhvika Jewellers has been at the forefront of jewelry excellence, drawing inspiration from ancient goldsmith traditions while embracing modern innovation. Our commitment to quality and artistry has made us a trusted name in luxury jewelry. Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted by master artisans who have inherited centuries-old techniques, ensuring that every creation is not just jewelry, but a work of art that tells a timeless story.
